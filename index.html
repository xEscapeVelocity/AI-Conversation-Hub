Why use Council?

A group chat designed explicitly to help you make decisions, compare perspectives, and orchestrate AI workflows in real time.

Consensus Group Chat

Bring Gemini, Claude, Llama 3, and more into a single conversation room. Watch them comment, refine, and build on each other's responses.

Auto-Debate Mode

Type a single prompt, click start, and watch your configured AI agents debate, collaborate, and execute multi-turn conversations automatically.

100% Client-Side Privacy

All API keys, configurations, and chat histories are stored locally inside your browser's local sandbox or desktop app. No servers, no tracking.

Smart Rate-Limit Management

Excludes rate-limited models automatically with a 10-minute cooldown or manual override, keeping your roundtables completely seamless.

Local Ollama / LM Studio

Scan and auto-discover your local Ollama or LM Studio endpoints with a single click. Chat with fully offline, local models for free.

Workspace Backups

Easily import and export your entire workspace setup (all history, configurations, and settings) in a single JSON backup file.

Get Council. Today

Run Council as a standalone client on your computer or install it directly onto your Android device.

Windows Client

Install the native desktop client compiled via Tauri for maximum performance and direct integration with local APIs.

  • Download the .exe installer from our releases.
  • Double-click the installer and complete the setup.
  • Open Council and connect your models.

Android Client

Take your AI consensus roundtables on the go with the native Android APK package compiled using Capacitor.

  • Download the app-debug.apk package file.
  • Install it on your device (enable installation from unknown sources).
  • Launch Council directly from your App Drawer.
